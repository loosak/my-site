Astro's islands architecture isn't just a clever idea — it's the first mental model
that actually matches how I think about content-heavy sites.

<Callout type="info">
  Zero JS by default means your lighthouse score starts at 100 and you
  deliberately opt *in* to interactivity, not opt out.
</Callout>

## The content layer

The new content layer in v5+ is a genuine step change. Type-safe frontmatter,
`getCollection`, and now remote loaders mean you can pull from any CMS without
giving up the local-dev experience.

```ts
const posts = await getCollection('blog', ({ data }) => !data.draft);
```

## What I miss from Next

Honestly? Not much. Parallel routes were nice. That's about it.
